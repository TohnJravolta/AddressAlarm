On-device safety alerts for risky addresses

AddressAlarm keeps a private list of locations you want to avoid or approach with caution. When a monitored app displays a matching address, AddressAlarm surfaces a clear warning so you stay aware in high-stakes moments.

Download APK (coming soon)

Why AddressAlarm?

Private & offline

All matching happens locally on your device. No servers, no tracking, and no data leaves your phone.

Custom watchlists

Flag addresses with notes and tags so you remember why a location is risky, from aggressive pets to restricted zones.

Smart alerts

Debounced notifications prevent alert fatigue, while per-app controls keep the overlay focused where you need it.

Import & export

Back up your data or share watchlists with trusted teammates using built-in import/export tools.

How it works

  1. Download the APK from the latest GitHub release.
  2. Install the app and enable the AddressAlarm accessibility service.
  3. Add addresses, tags, and notes to your personal watchlist.
  4. Select the navigation or gig apps you want AddressAlarm to monitor.
  5. When an address matches, AddressAlarm raises a clear, dismissible warning overlay.

Need more help? Follow the installation guide or read the full documentation.

Privacy & safety first

Your data, your device

AddressAlarm stores your watchlist locally and never transmits it elsewhere.

Assistive alerts

The app provides reminders so you can make informed decisions—it never auto-declines or accepts work for you.
